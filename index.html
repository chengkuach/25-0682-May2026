About Us

Cheng Real Estates is a trusted real estate company dedicated to helping individuals, families, and businesses find the perfect property across Kenya. We specialize in property sales, rentals, property management, and real estate investment advisory services. Our team is committed to providing professional, honest, and reliable services to ensure that every client enjoys a smooth and successful property experience.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellence by connecting clients with quality residential homes, apartments, commercial spaces, and investment properties in prime locations. We understand that purchasing or renting a property is one of the most important decisions a person can make, which is why we work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide the best possible solutions.

At Cheng Real Estates, our mission is to deliver value, transparency, and customer satisfaction while helping our clients achieve their real estate goals. Whether you are buying, selling, renting, or investing, we are your trusted partner every step of the way.

Our vision is to become one of Kenya’s leading real estate agencies by providing innovative property solutions, maintaining high ethical standards, and building long-term relationships with our clients. We strive to make property ownership and investment accessible, secure, and rewarding for everyone.
